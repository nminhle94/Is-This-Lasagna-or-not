Chocolate Sauce

Let's make chocolate sauce together. Let's get started!

  1. In large pot, add cream to pot and vanilla beans.
  2. Once cream is at 145F, slowly add 1/4C of chocolate chips and whisk.
  3. After fully melted, add 1/2C of chocolate chips at a time.
  4. Add chips until desired consistency is reached.
  5. ENJOY!!!!!
