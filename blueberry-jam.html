Blueberry Jam

This is how you make Blueberry Jam. Let's get started!


  1. Get large pot and add blueberries in pot.
  2. Put powdered sugar and 2Tbsp of water into pot with blueberries.
  3. Bring to boil and then lower heat to simmer.
  4. After jam is nape on back of spoon, cool and store properly.
  5. ENJOY!!!
